Track Your Mood and Feelings Daily

Use the app as a personal mood journal to log your mood every day. With just a few taps, you can track how you feel and create a clear emotional record over time. Daily tracking helps you notice emotional patterns and understand what truly affects your mental state.

Express Yourself with Journaling and Photos

Self-expression is at the core of this mood tracker. Write short journal entries, capture moments with a photo, and turn your emotions into something tangible. This combination of text and visuals makes emotional reflection natural and engaging.
