Are you looking for the meaning of your dream? You've come to the right place. Our Dream Book is a modern, organized collection of dream symbols that combines classic symbolic knowledge with a modern psychological approach and Artificial Intelligence analysis. Our alphabetical dream dictionary is designed so that you can find the key motifs that appeared in your subconscious during your night's rest in just a few seconds.

How to use the alphabetical dream dictionary?

Choose the first letter of the symbol you remember from your dream (e.g., "D" for dog, "F" for flying). Remember that dreams should rarely be taken literally. Often, the most important thing is not the object itself, but the emotions that accompanied it. If you dreamed of escaping, check not only the entry for "escape," but also symbols like a corridor, a person chasing you, or catching.

Why is it worth checking dream meanings alphabetically?

Alphabetical order is the simplest and fastest way to find answers. Every letter in our Dream Book contains carefully crafted interpretations. We don't limit ourselves to one-sentence definitions from years ago. Each entry is a deep analysis of:

  • The general meaning of the symbol in culture and psychology (e.g., according to Carl Jung or Sigmund Freud).
  • The impact of emotions on interpretation (the same symbol seen in joy versus in fear means something completely different).
  • Life guidance that your subconscious is trying to convey to you in your waking life.
